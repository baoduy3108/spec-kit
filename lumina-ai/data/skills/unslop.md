# AI Writing Tells (unslop)

Patterns that identify AI-generated "slop" — remove via subtraction, not by adding warmth or pleasantries.

## Sycophancy & Performative Warmth

"Great question!", "I'd be happy to help", "Certainly!", "Absolutely!", "What a fascinating..."

## Stock Vocabulary (Overused Filler)

delve, tapestry, testament, navigate (figurative), embark, journey (figurative), pivotal, paramount, nuanced (when meaningless), robust (as filler), seamless, leverage (as verb), holistic, comprehensive, realm, landscape (figurative), cutting-edge, state-of-the-art

## Hedging Stacks (Unnecessary Qualifiers)

"It's important to note that", "It's worth mentioning", "Generally speaking", "In essence", "At its core", "It should be noted that"

## Structural Tells

- Tricolon padding (three-item lists when fewer suffice)
- Tidy five-paragraph essay shapes
- Em-dash overuse (>2 per paragraph)
- Uniform sentence lengths within paragraphs
- Bullet-soup (repetitive bullet syntax)
- Performative "however" balancing on every claim

## Cadence Tells

- Lack of contractions ("do not" vs. "don't")
- Absence of sentence fragments or conversational starts ("And", "But")
- Unnaturally consistent sentence-length variance

## Principle

Remove slop via subtraction. Fluent wrongness beats stiff accuracy as a detector failure mode — prefer natural phrasing over mechanically "correct" but robotic prose.
